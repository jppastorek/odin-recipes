Mojo Pork

Description

My mojo pork is simply a pulled pork recipe but with mojo flavors of garlic, citrus, and mint.

Ingredients

Serves 8-10

  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 7 lb pork butt
  • lots of garlic
  • 2 oranges
  • 2 limes
  • a few sprigs of mint
  • oregano
  • cumin

Steps

  1. Rub the pork with cumin oregano, salt and pepper. Marinate the pork overnight in the orange, limes, and garlic.
  2. In a large Dutch oven, heat some oil and then brown the pork on all sides.
  3. Chop the onions.
  4. Pour the leftover marinade in the pot. Put the pork in the pot with chopped onions on top.
  5. Close top and cook in the oven at 225.
  6. Periodically check tenderness of pork. It should be easily pulled apart with a fork. Takes about 1 hour per pound.
  7. When finished, pull the pork with forks and remove from the pot. Drizzle some of the juice onto the pork.
  8. Serve with congris and some platanos.
