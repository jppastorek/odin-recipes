Congris

Description

Congris (aka moros y cristianos) is simply black beans and rice, but the rice is cooked with the black bean soaking water.

Ingredients

Serves 8-10

  • 2 yellow onions
  • 2 bell peppers
  • lots of garlic
  • ham stock (enough to fill the pot)
  • 2 lbs dry black beans
  • 3 cups white rice
  • oregano
  • cumin
  • bay leaves

Steps

  1. (OPTIONAL) Prepare the stock - see stock recipe
  2. Soak the beans overnight. Save some of the water
  3. Chop all the vegetables
  4. Heat your pot to medium heat and saute the vegetables.
  5. Add garlic. Saute until fragrant.
  6. Add some stock.
  7. Add the beans.
  8. Add more stock until the beans are covered by about 2 inches of water.
  9. Add bay leaves, oregano, and cumin.
  10. Simmer 2-3 hours. Simmer with top off, cooking off the water.
  11. Cook the rice with the soaked bean water.
  12. Once rice and beans are ready, mix beans into the rice until you have a good balance.
